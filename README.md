# The Vault

### The Smart Way to Track Your Net Worth.

**Stop guessing. Start knowing.**
The Vault is the personal finance companion designed for modern life. Whether you hold cash, foreign currency or gold, bring all your scattered assets into one beautiful, secure dashboard. No spreadsheets, no confusion. Just total financial clarity.

---

## Why The Vault?

We built The Vault because tracking your wealth shouldn't be a chore.

### Total Clarity at a Glance
Forget checking five different banking apps. See your total net worth and asset distribution in a single, elegantly designed dashboard.

### Effortless Portfolio Management
Add your assets in seconds. Whether it's a stash of USD under the mattress or Gold in the bank, inputting your data is intuitive and fast.

### Live Market Intelligence
Your money never sleeps, and neither do we. The app automatically pulls the latest exchange rates and gold prices to calculate the real-time value of your holdings.

### Stay Agile
Spent some cash? Bought more gold? Update your balances with a single tap. Keeping your records accurate has never been this easy.
